My name is Mark Angelo Zaragoza Marquez.

I was born inside the coziest house as I must say, near the shore of Baranggay Big Tabinay, Puerto Galera, Oriental Mindoro.

The parents that brought me to this world are not the perfect parents yet have provided for me until the end of my senior high school days. An ultra industrious mother that sometimes even sacrifices her health due to overwork, Adelfa Marquez. A hard-working yet have some self problems and self-issues father, Manolito Marquez.

I have three siblings namely Lloyd Ian Marquez (five years younger to me), Aldrin James Marquez (10 years younger to me), and the only girl Jammela Marquez (13 years younger to me). I think I can say that I have bonded with them properly before I moved from Puerto Galera to Batangas for college.

My friends say that I am a prodigy yet not. I think they say that because there are many things that interests me, and if I have the will to learn it, I am able to learn it quickly. The list of the things that interests me and sometimes can be included in my hobbies are:

Drawing

Rubik's Cubing

Pen spinning and tapping (a little bit)

I can say that my life now is not as good as I was a kid.

I was a very happy and playful person as a kid. I can remember that I really enjoyed being with many people, I talk to them as a child, I laughed with them. I enjoyed running under the sun at the hot sand near the shore.

It was all just a memory now.

I don't really remember when it happened but I suddenly changed. I began being afraid of talking to people. I became shy. I became a loner. I have always questioned my self, "What is my purpose in life? Why am I living?"

Until now, sometimes, I ask myself those questions. However, I am contented at what and who I have now. I am happy. I learned how to live life because of the people I've met as my life goes on.

As of now, I am a college student at Batangas State University - Alangilan Campus. I am a 3rd-year Information Technology student Major in Service Management. I am currently boarding at a dormitory near the school which is conviniently five or less minutes in walking distance.

I am managing to build my responsibility since I am away from my family which is somewhat challenging but I think I will be able to get used to it. Also, I recently notice that I am little by little bit building confidence as time passes by since I am able to talk casually to my boardmates and to my new classmates.

I can say that I am satisfied as of now.
